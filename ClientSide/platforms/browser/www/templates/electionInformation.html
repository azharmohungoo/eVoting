Useful Information


In national elections you vote for members of Parliament and in provincial elections (held on the same day as national elections) you vote for members of the provincial legislature.

In municipal elections you are voting for city or town councillors who will run your town council for the next five years.

In national and provincial elections, you vote for a political party (Proportional Representative or PR electoral system) to get seats in the national and provincial legislatures.
